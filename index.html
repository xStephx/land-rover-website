Overview
What is it?

The 2022 Land Rover Defender blends iconic off-road capability with modern sophistication. It offers powerful engine options, including a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four and a 3.0-liter mild-hybrid inline-six, ensuring robust performance. Advanced features like Terrain Response 2 and All-Terrain Progress Control enhance its ability to tackle challenging terrains with ease.

Inside, the Defender combines durability with luxury, featuring high-quality materials and the intuitive Pivi Pro infotainment system with a 10-inch touchscreen. Practical and versatile, it offers ample cargo space and various seating configurations, making it ideal for adventurers and families alike.
